FLEET OVERVIEW

Our own fleet of aircraft includes six freighter aircraft B747-400Fs ‘Queen of the Skies’, and passenger aircraft A330-200 & B757-200

The Boeing 747 -400F is a freighter aircraft which can hold up to 24,115 cubic feet (683 m³) of cargo and has a maximum payload of 108 metric tons.

The A330-200 is our premium passenger aircraft which flies business executives, military troops, holiday goers, and sports team across the globe.

With all the latest inflight entertainment and digital service offerings, the Boeing 757-200 offers our customers a personalized, comfortable and exclusive journey to their preferred destination.
